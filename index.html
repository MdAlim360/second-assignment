History


1977


W. Michael Felder, accomplished architect and project manager,
flexes his entrepreneurship skills by opening
Enviro Design as a sole proprietorship.


2003


Michael Felder expands his business. The company graduates to a corporation.
The name of the business changes from Enviro Design
to Enviro Design Architects, LLC.


2010


Founder and President W. Michael Felder, turns the reigns of leadership of Enviro Design Architects
over to his daughter Tenika Felder, AIA. Vice President Tenika Felder makes the decision
to rename the company to Redlef (Felder spelled backwards) Group Architects, LLC.



PHILOSOPHY

Redlef Group Architects' primary goal is to provide thoughtful and dedicated professional service to its clients by
producing the highest level of design quality possible while adhering to our clients' tastes and budget.
